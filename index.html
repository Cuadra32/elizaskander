My story

Fitness from the
inside out

Hi, I'm Eliza. I arrived in Geneva in 2013 and have been training clients here ever since. Before that, I toured Europe for 11 years as a professional dancer — which gave me a deep understanding of movement, body mechanics, and the discipline it takes to perform at your best.

I hold a CYQ Level 3 Personal Training qualification, a Nutrition Diploma from TCMA, and a Pre & Post Natal fitness certificate — so whether you're training for performance, a life event, or recovery after pregnancy, I have the background to support you properly.

I work with clients in English, French, Polish and Russian — so language is never a barrier.
