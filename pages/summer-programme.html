The 8-week plan

A structured progression that builds week by week — not a crash programme, but a real transformation.

  • 1–2

    Foundation

    Assess starting point, establish movement patterns, set up nutrition baseline. Moderate intensity.

  • 3–4

    Build

    Increase training volume. Introduce HIIT elements. Nutrition dialled in and tracking habits formed.

  • 5–6

    Push

    Peak training intensity. Metabolic conditioning. Nutrition refined around energy demands.

  • 7–8

    Refine & reveal

    Targeted work on specific areas. Nutrition periodisation for maximum definition. Final push.
