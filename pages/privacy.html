Privacy Policy

How elizaskander.com collects, uses and protects your personal data.

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who we are

This website is operated by Eliza Skander, personal trainer and nutrition coach based in Geneva, Switzerland. Contact: elizaskander@yahoo.com · Rue du Grand-Pré 58, 1202 Genève.

2. What data we collect

We only collect data that you voluntarily provide to us via the contact form on this website. This includes:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The content of your message (your goals and the type of session you're interested in)

We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising or analytics. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking tools.

3. How we use your data

The information you provide via the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry. Specifically:

  • To reply to your message about personal training or nutrition coaching
  • To arrange and manage personal training sessions if you become a client

We will never sell, share or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data is not used for marketing without your explicit consent.

4. Where your data is stored

This website is hosted on Netlify (USA). Contact form submissions are processed and stored by Netlify Forms. Netlify is GDPR compliant. You can read Netlify's privacy policy at netlify.com/privacy.

5. How long we keep your data

Contact form submissions are retained only for as long as is necessary to manage the enquiry or ongoing client relationship. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing elizaskander@yahoo.com.

6. Your rights (Swiss nLPD)

Under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nLPD), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact: elizaskander@yahoo.com

7. YouTube embeds

This website embeds videos from YouTube (Google LLC). When you watch an embedded video, YouTube may set cookies on your device and collect data in accordance with Google's privacy policy. We have no control over this. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

8. Google Maps

This website uses an embedded Google Maps iframe. When you view the map, Google may collect data in accordance with their privacy policy. See above for the link.

9. External links

This website contains links to external sites including Mels Fit (melsfit.ch) and Facebook. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites and recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update. Continued use of the website following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact:
